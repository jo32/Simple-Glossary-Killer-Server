Welcome using Simple Glossary Killer (Api)

author : Jolam (http://bandj.us/jo)

Introduction :

The Simple Glossary Killer is, just as its name implies, a app mainly for Chinese that helps u to learn your glossary, which support multi-platform (Windows xp, vista, 7, linux and all other platforms that support JRE).

Setup:

  • step 1 : Installing JRE (Java Runtime Environment)
    go to : http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/index.html
    and click "download JRE" then download the JRE suitable for u.(for me, Windows x86 Offline)

  • step 2 : Registering a new account.
    copy this url to your browser : http://gk.bandj.us/Register?username={username}&password={username}
    and replace {username} and {password} with username and password u desire.

  • step 3.1 : Downloading Simple Glossary Killer
    simply download this : http://cid-5bfa911f69cb4c0d.office.live.com/self.aspx/public/sgk.zip
    and decompress the .zip file to wherever u like.

  • step 3.2 : Updating Simple Glossary Killer (important)
    simply download this : http://cid-5bfa911f69cb4c0d.office.live.com/self.aspx/public/sgk.jar
    and replace the sgk.jar in your sgk.zip decompression directory.

  • step 4 : Uploading your word list via the app.
    the word list file is a text file(usually ends with .txt) that its content is one word per line.
    here is a sample : newestGre.txt from iciba.net.

How-to-use :

  • right arrow → ：go to the next word.
  • left arrow → ：go to the previous word.
  • keypad 1-5 ：rate the word with corresponding point.
  • keypad enter ：get the meaning of the word form dict.cn.
